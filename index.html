The Four Laws

Rules of Existence

01

Underpopulation

A live cell with fewer than 2 live neighbours dies.

02

Survival

A live cell with 2 or 3 live neighbours lives on.

03

Overpopulation

A live cell with more than 3 live neighbours dies.

04

Reproduction

A dead cell with exactly 3 live neighbours becomes alive.

Getting Started

How to Play

STEP 01

Draw Your Pattern

Tap or drag across the grid to toggle cells alive or dead and create your seed.

STEP 02

Start the Simulation

Press play and watch each generation unfold from the four simple rules.

STEP 03

Observe & Iterate

Pause, step forward, adjust speed, and load classic patterns from the menu.
